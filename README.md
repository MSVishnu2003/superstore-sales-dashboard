# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, product trends, and geographic sales distribution using the Superstore dataset.

## Business Objective

Provide decision-makers with a centralized dashboard to monitor key business metrics and identify revenue and profit opportunities.

## Tools Used

- Tableau Public
- Data Visualization
- Dashboard Design
- KPI Reporting

## Key Metrics

- Total Sales: $2.3M
- Total Profit: $292K
- Total Orders: 5,111

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Trend Analysis
Tracks monthly sales performance and seasonal trends.

### Sales by Category
Compares revenue contribution across product categories.

### Profit Map
Visualizes profit performance by geographic region.

### Top Products Analysis
Identifies highest-performing products based on sales.

## Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales among categories.
- Significant regional variations exist in profitability.
- A small group of products contributes a large portion of total revenue.
- Sales peaked during the final quarter of the year.
